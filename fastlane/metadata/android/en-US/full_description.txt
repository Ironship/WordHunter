Word Hunter Pocket is a reader and vocabulary-learning workspace for Android.

Import text, EPUB files, subtitles, and PDFs that contain a text layer. Words
selected while reading can be saved with their translation and source context,
then reviewed with flashcards and spaced repetition.

Books, vocabulary, settings, and progress are stored locally. A folder selected
by the user can be used to synchronize data with Word Hunter on a computer.

No account is required. Optional features such as public-domain book discovery,
online dictionaries, translation providers, Wikipedia image search, YouTube
subtitles, and YouGlish pronunciation examples contact third-party services when
used.

Scanned PDFs require OCR in the desktop version before they are synchronized to
Pocket.
